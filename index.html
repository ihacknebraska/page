The mission

Grow cyber talent and strengthen local resilience.

iHackNebraska brings together students, professionals, businesses, educators, and security enthusiasts to learn by doing. The initiative creates a collaborative environment where responsible hacking, research, mentorship, and innovation can thrive.

By combining education, community outreach, and practical cyber defense exercises, iHackNebraska aims to help Nebraska become a regional leader in cybersecurity talent and readiness.

How it works

Training, mentorship, and real-world practice.

  1. Learn

    Beginner-friendly and advanced workshops covering security fundamentals, tools, defense, and responsible disclosure.

  2. Practice

    Community labs, red-team and blue-team exercises, CTF challenges, and guided research sprints.

  3. Serve

    Projects and readiness support for local organizations that need practical help navigating emerging threats.

Community
